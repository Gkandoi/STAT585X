GOexplore: A shiny app to visualize Gene Ontology Annotations

Gene Ontology

Provides controlled vocabularies of defined terms representing gene product properties

Three aspects:

  • Cellular Component: component of a cell
  • Molecular Function: molecular level elemental activities
  • Biological Process: series of one or more molecular events

Gene Ontology Annotations

Assigning GO terms to genes

Supported by evidence codes

  • Experimental
  • Computational
  • Author Statements
  • Curatorial Statements
  • Automatic

Availability

The Gene Ontology Consortium: http://www.geneontology.org/page/download-annotations
